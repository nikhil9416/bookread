29 Arrays and linked lists Inserting into the middle of a list Suppose you want your todo list to work more like a calendar. Earlier, you were adding things to the end of the list. Now you want to add them in the order in which they should be done. What’s better if you want to insert elements in the middle: arrays or lists? With lists, it’s as easy as changing what the previous element points to. But for arrays, you have to shiﬅ all the rest of the elements down.
