39 Recursion Here’s one approach. 1. Make a pile of boxes to look through. 2. Grab a box, and look through it. 3. If you ﬁnd a box, add it to the pile to look through later. 4. If you ﬁnd a key, you’re done! 5. Repeat. Here’s an alternate approach. 1. Look through the box. 2. If you ﬁnd a box, go to step 1. 3. If you ﬁnd a key, you’re done!
