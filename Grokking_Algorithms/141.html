Finally, remember our conversation about directed versus undirected graphs from chapter 6? An undirected graph means that both nodes point to each other. at’s a cycle! With an undirected graph, each edge adds another cycle. Dijkstra’s algorithm only works with directed acyclic graphs, called DAGs for short.
