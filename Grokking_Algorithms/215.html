You have a set of features: • Weather on a scale of 1 to 5 (1 = bad, 5 = great). • Weekend or holiday? (1 if it’s a weekend or a holiday, 0 otherwise.) • Is there a game on? (1 if yes, 0 if no.) And you know how many loaves of bread you’ve sold in the past for diﬀerent sets of features.
