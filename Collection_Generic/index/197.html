To compare two PriorityTasks, we first compare their priorities; if the priorities are unequal, the higher priority tasks comes first, and if the priorities are equal, we use the natural ordering on the underlying tasks. To test whether two PriorityTasks are equal, we check whether they have the same priority and the same task. These definitions ensure that the natural ordering is consistent with equals (see Section 3.1).
