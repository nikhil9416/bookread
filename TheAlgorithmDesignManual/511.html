It is a surprising fact that no matter how the boys and girls rate each other, there is always at least one stable marriage. Further, such a marriage can be found in O(n 2 ) time [GS62]. An important application of stable marriage occurs in the annual matching of medical residents to hospitals. The maximum matching is equal in size to the minimum vertex cover in bipartite graphs. This implies that both the minimum vertex cover problem and maximum inde- pendent set problems can be solved in polynomial time on bipartite graphs.
