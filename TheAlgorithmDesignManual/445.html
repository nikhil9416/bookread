14. COMBINATORIAL PROBLEMS 435 • Knuth [Knu97a, Knu98] – The standard reference on searching and sorting, with signiﬁcant material on combinatorial objects such as permutations. New material on the generation of permutations [Knu05a], subsets and partitions [Knu05b], and trees [Knu06] has been released on “fasciles,”—short paper- back chunks of what is slated to be the mythical Volume 4. • Stanton and White [SW86a] – An undergraduate combinatorics text with al- gorithms for generating permutations, subsets, and set partitions. It contains relevant programs in Pascal. • Pemmaraju and Skiena [PS03] – This description of Combinatorica, a library of over 400 Mathematica functions for generating combinatorial objects and graph theory (see Section 19.1.9), provides a distinctive view of how diﬀerent algorithms can ﬁt together. Its second author is considered qualiﬁed to write a manual on algorithm design.
