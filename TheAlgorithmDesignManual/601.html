17.8 INTERSECTION DETECTION 591 INPUT OUTPUT 17.8 Intersection Detection Input description: A set S of lines and line segments l 1 ,...,l n or a pair of polygons or polyhedra P 1 and P 2 . Problem description: Which pairs of line segments intersect each other? What is the intersection of P 1 and P 2 ? Discussion: Intersection detection is a fundamental geometric primitive with many applications. Picture a virtual-reality simulation of an architectural model for a building. The illusion of reality vanishes the instant the virtual person walks through a virtual wall. To enforce such physical constraints, any such intersection between polyhedral models must be immediately detected and the operator notiﬁed or constrained. Another application of intersection detection is design rule checking for VLSI layouts. A minor design defect resulting in two crossing metal strips could short out the chip, but such errors can be detected before fabrication, using programs to ﬁnd all intersections between line segments.
