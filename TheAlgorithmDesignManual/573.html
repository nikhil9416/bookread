There is a growing body of implementations of geometric algorithms. We point out speciﬁc implementations where applicable in the catalog, but the reader should be particularly aware of CGAL (Computational Geometry Algorithms Library)— a comprehensive library of geometric algorithms in C++ produced as a result of a joint European project. Anyone with a serious interest in geometric computing should check it out at http://www.cgal.org/.
