GRAPH PROBLEMS: POLYNOMIAL-TIME • Ahuja, Magnanti, and Orlin [AMO93] – While purporting to be a book on network ﬂows, it covers the gamut of graph algorithms with an emphasis on operations research. Strongly recommended. • Gibbons [Gib85] – A good book on graph algorithms, including planarity test- ing, matching, and Eulerian/Hamiltonian cycles, as well as more elementary topics. • Even [Eve79a] – An older but still respected advanced text on graph al- gorithms, with a particularly thorough treatment of planarity-testing algo- rithms.
