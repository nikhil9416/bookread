Problem description: Find the shortest route taking s to t without intersecting any obstacles. Discussion: That motion planning is a complex problem is obvious to anyone who has tried to move a large piece of furniture into a small apartment. It also arises in systems for molecular docking. Many drugs are small molecules that act by binding to a given target model. Identifying which binding sites are accessible to a candidate drug is clearly an instance of motion planning. Plotting paths for mobile robots is another canonical motion-planning application. Finally, motion planning provides a tool for computer animation. Given the set of object models and where they appear in scenes s 1 and s 2 , a motion planning algorithm can construct a short sequence of intermediate motions to transform s 1 to s 2 .
