9.11 EXERCISES 355 9-30. [5] A vertex coloring of graph G =(V,E) is an assignment of colors to vertices of V such that each edge (x, y) implies that vertices x and y are assigned diﬀerent colors. Give an algorithm for vertex coloring G using at most Δ + 1 colors, where Δ is the maximum vertex degree of G.
