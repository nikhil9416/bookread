Similar procedure computes lower hull. Stitch these together to form the convex hull. Figure 9-3 shows Convex Hull Scan in action as it computes the partial upper hull. Note that the overall approach makes numerous mistakes as it visits every point in P from left to right, yet it adjusts by dropping—sometimes repeatedly—the middle of the last three points while it correctly computes the upper partial hull.
