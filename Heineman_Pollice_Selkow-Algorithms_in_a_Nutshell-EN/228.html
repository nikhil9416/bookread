Although all three searches have the potential to explore an exponential number of states, A*Search explores the smallest number given an admissible h(n) estima‐ tion function. There are other known ways to solve n 2 – 1 sliding tile puzzles besides relying on path finding. One ingenious approach proposed by Parberry (1995) is to use a Divide and Conquer strategy. That is, given an n×n puzzle, where n > 3, first com‐ plete the leftmost column and topmost row and then recursively solve the resulting (n − 1) 2 − 1 puzzle.
