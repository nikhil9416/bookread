Once the nested for loops compute the maximum value, m[n][W], the subsequent while loop shows how to recover the actual items selected by “walk‐ ing” over the m matrix. It starts at the lower righthand corner, m[n][W], and deter‐ mines whether the i th item was selected, based on whether m[i][w] is different from m[i-1][w]. If this is the case, it records the selection and then moves left in m by removing the weight of i and continuing until it hits the first row (no more items) or the left column (no more weight); otherwise it tries the previous item.
