384 CHAPTER 14 Working with RSocket Summary  RSocket is an asynchronous binary protocol that offers four communication models: request-response, request-stream, fire-and-forget, and channel.  Spring supports RSocket on the server through controllers and handler meth- ods annotated with @MessageHandler.  The RSocketRequester enables client-side communication with RSocket.  In both cases, Spring’s RSocket support works through Reactor’s Flux and Mono reactive types for fully reactive communication.  RSocket communication takes place over TCP by default but can also be trans- ported over WebSocket to deal with firewall constraints and browser clients.
