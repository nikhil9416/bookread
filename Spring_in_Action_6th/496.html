A.4 Initializing a project at start.spring.io Although one of the IDE-based initialization options described thus far will likely suit your needs, it’s possible that you may use a completely different IDE, or you might favor working with a simpler text editor. In that case, you can still take advantage of the Spring Initializr using the Initializr web-based interface. To get started, direct your favorite web browser to https://start.spring.io.
