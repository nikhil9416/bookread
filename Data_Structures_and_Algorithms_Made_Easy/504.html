10.1 What is Sorting? Sorting is an algorithm that arranges the elements of a list in a certain order [either ascending or descending]. The output is a permutation or reordering of the input. 10.2 Why is Sorting Necessary? Sorting is one of the important categories of algorithms in computer science and a lot of research has gone into this category.
