Just after the k–1 th iteration, A k–1 [i,j ] = smallest length of any path from vertex i to vertex j that does not pass through the vertices {k + 1, k + 2,.... n}. That means, it passes through the vertices possibly through {1,2,3,..., k– 1}. In each iteration, the value A [i ][j ] is updated with minimum of A k–1 [i ,j ] and A k–1 [i , k]+A k – 1 [k,j ].
