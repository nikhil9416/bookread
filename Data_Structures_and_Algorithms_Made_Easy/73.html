Sample Call: Problem-6 Solve the recurrence T(n) = 2T(n – 1) + 2 n . Solution: At each level of the recurrence tree, the number of problems is double from the previous level, while the amount of work being done in each problem is half from the previous level. Formally, the i th level has 2 i problems, each requiring 2 n–i work. Thus the i th level requires exactly 2 n work. The depth of this tree is n, because at the i th level, the originating call will be T(n – i ). Thus the total complexity for T(n) is T(n2 n ).
