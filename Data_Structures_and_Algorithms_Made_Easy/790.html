Problem-42 Maximum sum sub-matrix: Given an n × n matrix M of positive and negative integers, give an algorithm to find the sub-matrix with the largest possible sum. Solution: Let Aux[r, c] represent the sum of rectangular subarray of M with one corner at entry [1,1] and the other at [r,c]. Since there are n 2 such possibilities, we can compute them in O(n 2 ) time. After computing all possible sums, the sum of any rectangular subarray of M can be computed in constant time. This gives an O(n 4 ) algorithm: we simply guess the lower-left and the upper-right corner of the rectangular subarray and use the Aux table to compute its sum.
