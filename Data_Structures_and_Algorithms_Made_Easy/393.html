There is an O(logm × logn) algorithm for merging two binary heaps with sizes m and n. For m= n, this algorithm takes O(log 2 n) time complexity. We will be skipping it due to its difficulty and scope. For better merging performance, we can use another variant of binary heap like a Fibonacci- Heap which can merge in O(1) on average (amortized).
