Time Complexity: Time for sorting + Time for searching = O(Max(mlogm, nlogn)). Space Complexity: O(Max(m, n)). Problem-23 Can we improve the complexity for Problem-17? Solution: Yes. Efficient Approach: • Find lengths (L1 and L2) of both lists - O(n) + O(m) = O(max(m, n)). • Take the difference d of the lengths -- O(1). • Make d steps in longer list -- O(d). • Step in both lists in parallel until links to next node match -- O(min(m, n)). • Total time complexity = O(max(m, n)). • Space Complexity = O(1).
