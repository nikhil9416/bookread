Problem-4 Suppose you are given an array s[1...n] and a procedure reverse (s,i,j) which reverses the order of elements in between positions i and j (both inclusive). What does the following sequence a) Rotates s left by k positions b) Leaves s unchanged c) Reverses all elements of s d) None of the above Solution: (b). Effect of the above 3 reversals for any k is equivalent to left rotation of the array of size n by k [refer Problem-3].
