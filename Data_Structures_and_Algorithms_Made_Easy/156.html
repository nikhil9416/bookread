Problem-51 Median in an infinite series of integers Solution: Median is the middle number in a sorted list of numbers (if we have an odd number of elements). If we have an even number of elements, the median is the average of two middle numbers in a sorted list of numbers. We can solve this problem with linked lists (with both sorted and unsorted linked lists). First , let us try with an unsorted linked list. In an unsorted linked list, we can insert the element either at the head or at the tail. The disadvantage with this approach is that finding the median takes O(n).
