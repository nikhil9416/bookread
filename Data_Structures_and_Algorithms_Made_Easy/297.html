Example: Assume that one symbol is read at a time. If the symbol is an operand, we create a tree node and push a pointer to it onto a stack. If the symbol is an operator, pop pointers to two trees T 1 and T 2 from the stack (T 1 is popped first) and forma new tree whose root is the operator and whose left and right children point to T 2 and T 1 respectively. A pointer to this new tree is then pushed onto the stack.
