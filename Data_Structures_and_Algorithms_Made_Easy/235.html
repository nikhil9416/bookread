Time Complexity: O(n). Space Complexity: O(n). InOrder Traversal In Inorder Traversal the root is visited between the subtrees. Inorder traversal is defined as follows: • Traverse the left subtree in Inorder. • Visit the root. • Traverse the right subtree in Inorder.
