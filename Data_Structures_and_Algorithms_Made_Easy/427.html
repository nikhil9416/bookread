• Definitions that we use: ○ Directed edge: ▪ ordered pair of vertices (u, v) ▪ first vertex u is the origin ▪ second vertex v is the destination ▪ Example: one-way road traffic ○ Undirected edge: ▪ unordered pair of vertices (u, v) ▪ Example: railway lines ○ Directed graph: ▪ all the edges are directed ▪ Example: route network ○ Undirected graph: ▪ all the edges are undirected ▪ Example: flight network
