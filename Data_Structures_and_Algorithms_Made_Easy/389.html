Problem-6 Show that the height of a heap with n elements is logn? Solution: A heap is a complete binary tree. All the levels, except the lowest, are completely full. A heap has at least 2 h elements and at most elements 2 h ≤ n ≤2 h+1 – 1. This implies, h ≤ logn ≤ h + 1. Since h is an integer, h = logn.
