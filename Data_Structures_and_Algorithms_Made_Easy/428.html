• When an edge connects two vertices, the vertices are said to be adjacent to each other and the edge is incident on both vertices. • A graph with no cycles is called a tree. Atree is an acyclic connected graph. • A self loop is an edge that connects a vertex to itself. • Two edges are parallel if they connect the same pair of vertices. • The Degree of a vertex is the number of edges incident on it. • A subgraph is a subset of a graph’s edges (with associated vertices) that form a graph. • A path in a graph is a sequence of adjacent vertices.
