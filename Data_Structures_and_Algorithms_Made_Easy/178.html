Important Properties • Let us consider the infix expression 2 + 3*4 and its postfix equivalent 234*+. Notice that between infix and postfix the order of the numbers (or operands) is unchanged. It is 2 3 4 in both cases. But the order of the operators * and + is affected in the two expressions. • Only one stack is enough to convert an infix expression to postfix expression. The stack that we use in the algorithm will be used to change the order of operators from infix to postfix. The stack we use will only contain operators and the open parentheses symbol ‘(‘. Postfix expressions do not contain parentheses. We shall not output the parentheses in the postfix output.
