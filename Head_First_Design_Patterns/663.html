Memento Uses and Drawbacks Keeping the single responsibility principle in mind, it’s also a good idea to keep the state that you’re saving separate from the key object. This separate object that holds the state is known as the Memento object. While this isn’t a terribly fancy implementation, notice that the Client has no access to the Memento’s data.
