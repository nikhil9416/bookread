xvi 6 Encapsulating Invocation In this chapter we take encapsulation to a whole new level: we’re going to encapsulate method invocation. That’s right, by encapsulating invocation we can crystallize pieces of computation so that the object invoking the computation doesn’t need to worry about how to do things; it just uses our crystallized method to get it done.
