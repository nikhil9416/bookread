Let’s take that and give our Waitress a method that can tell us exactly which items are vegetarian. print() is only called on MenuItems, never composites. Can you see why? The printVegetarianMenu() method takes the allMenu’s composite and gets its iterator. That will be our CompositeIterator. Iterate through every element of the composite. Call each element’s isVegetarian() method and if true, we call its print() method. We implemented isVegetarian() on the Menus to always throw an exception. If that happens we catch the exception, but continue with our iteration.
