44 Chapter 2 Meet the Observer Pattern You know how newspaper or magazine subscriptions work: A newspaper publisher goes into business and begins publishing newspapers. You subscribe to a particular publisher, and every time there’s a new edition it gets delivered to you. As long as you remain a subscriber, you get new newspapers. You unsubscribe when you don’t want papers anymore, and they stop being delivered.
