We have all the components of the deﬁnition: we have a problem, which includes the goal of getting to work, and the constraints of time, distance and probably some other factors. Right? I’ve been thinking about the three-part deﬁnition, and I don’t think it deﬁnes a pattern at all. We followed the Design Pattern deﬁnition and deﬁned a problem, a context, and a solution (which works!). Is this a pattern? If not, how did it fail?
