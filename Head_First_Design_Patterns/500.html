462 Chapter 11 Get ready for Virtual Proxy Okay, so far you’ve seen the deﬁnition of the Proxy Pattern and you’ve taken a look at one speciﬁc example: the Remote Proxy. Now we’re going to take a look at a different type of proxy, the Virtual Proxy. As you’ll discover, the Proxy Pattern can manifest itself in many forms, yet all the forms follow roughly the general proxy design. Why so many forms? Because the proxy pattern can be applied to a lot of different use cases.
