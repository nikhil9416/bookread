A Preview of Calculus CALCULUS IS FUNDAMENTALLY DIFFERENT FROM the mathematics that you have studied previ- ously: calculus is less static and more dynamic. It is concerned with change and motion; it deals with quantities that approach other quantities. For that reason it may be useful to have an overview of the subject before beginning its intensive study.
