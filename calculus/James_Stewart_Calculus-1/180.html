In fact, the computation of rates of change is important in all of the natural sciences, in engineering, and even in the social sciences. Further examples will be given in Section 3.7. All these rates of change are derivatives and can therefore be interpreted as slopes of tangents. This gives added signiicance to the solution of the tangent problem.
