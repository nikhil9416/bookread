In general, the moment of inertia plays much the same role in rotational motion that mass plays in linear motion. The moment of inertia of a wheel is what makes it difi- cult to start or stop the rotation of the wheel, just as the mass of a car is what makes it difﬁcult to start or stop the motion of the car.
