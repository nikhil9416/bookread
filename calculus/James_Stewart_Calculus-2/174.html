General Diferential Equations In general, a differential equation is an equation that contains an unknown function and one or more of its derivatives. The order of a differential equation is the order of the highest derivative that occurs in the equation. Thus Equations 1 and 2 are irst-order equations and Equation 3 is a second-order equation.
