In such cases the following theorem is useful. Although we state it for Type 1 integrals, a similar theorem is true for Type 2 integrals. Comparison Theorem Suppose that f and t are continuous functions with f s xd > ts xd > 0 for x > a. (a) If y ` a f s xd dx is convergent, then y ` a ts xd dx is convergent. (b) If y ` a ts xd dx is divergent, then y ` a f s xd dx is divergent. We omit the proof of the Comparison Theorem, but Figure 12 makes it seem plau- sible.
