Why Stream's laziness causes a sequential computation and how to avoid it The top half of figure 11.4 shows that processing the stream with a single pipeline implies the evaluation order (identified by the dotted line) is sequential. In fact, a new CompletableFuture is created only after the former one has been completely evaluated. Conversely, the bottom half of the figure demonstrates how gathering the CompletableFutures in a list first, represented by the oval, allows all of them to start before waiting for their completion.
