Which apple should have priority in the sorted list? You may want to provide a second Comparator to further refine the comparison. For example, after two apples are compared based on their weight, you may want to sort them by country of origin. The thenComparing method allows you to do just that. It takes a function as parameter (just like the method comparing) and provides a second Comparator if two objects are considered equal using the initial Comparator.
